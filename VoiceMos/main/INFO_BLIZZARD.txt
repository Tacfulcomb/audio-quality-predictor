Audio samples from the Blizzard Challenge are NOT included in this distribution
because it is not permitted to distribute them by their terms of use.  However,
running the scripts in included this distribution will download samples from the
Blizzard Challenge.  By running these scripts and downloading this data, you
agree to abide by the terms of use of the Blizzard data, which means that you
may NOT redistribute the audio samples.  For more information on the Blizzard
terms of use, please see:
https://www.cstr.ed.ac.uk/projects/blizzard/data.html

Data is gathered from Blizzard Challenges 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2013, and
2016.  Their citations are as follows:

V. Karaiskos, S. King, R. A. Clark, and C. Mayo, "The Blizzard Challenge 2008," in Proc. Blizzard Challenge Workshop, 2008.

A. W. Black, S. King, and K. Tokuda, "The Blizzard Challenge 2009," in Proc. Blizzard Challenge, 2009.

S. King and V. Karaiskos, "The Blizzard Challenge 2010," 2010.

S. King and V. Karaiskos, "The Blizzard Challenge 2011," 2011.

S. King and V. Karaiskos, "The Blizzard Challenge 2013," 2013.

S. King and V. Karaiskos, "The Blizzard Challenge 2016," 2016.
